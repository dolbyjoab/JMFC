Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to JollyMinou FC. This page explains how we collect and use information on our website.

What we collect

We do not store personal data on this site. Any information submitted through email links is sent directly to the club inbox.

How we use it

Contact messages are used only to respond to inquiries and recruitment requests. We will not sell your information or share it with third parties.

Cookies

Our site uses basic browser cookies only when required by your browser or for third-party services loaded from social networks. We do not use tracking cookies for marketing purposes.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, email us at jollyminoufc@gmail.com.
