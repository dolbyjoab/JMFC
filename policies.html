Club Policies

JollyMinou FC is committed to fair play, inclusion, and a respectful club community both on and off the field.

Recruitment policy

We recruit talent based on ability, commitment, and character. All applicants are evaluated fairly, with a focus on local development and team spirit.

Community code

Our club welcomes fans, families, and volunteers. Everyone is expected to treat players, staff, and matchday guests with respect, and to support a positive environment.

Safety & support

Safety is a priority. If any member of our club has a concern about behavior or wellbeing, they should contact club leadership immediately.

Club politics

JollyMinou FC focuses on sport, community development, and inclusion. We do not endorse divisive politics in our club spaces and encourage unity among supporters.
