Careers at JollyMinou FC

Looking for a role in football, event support, or community engagement? Join a club that plays hard and cares deeply about its people.

Open recruitment

We are currently recruiting players, matchday staff, and community partners for the season. If you want to help build Haitian football from inside the club, we want to hear from you.

  • Forward / Attacker — pace, finishing, and creative movement for club fixtures.
  • Midfield engine — tactical vision, physicality, and playmaking strength.
  • Defensive leader — strong tackling, aerial presence, and organization.
  • Goalkeeper — agile shot-stopper with leadership and quick distribution.
  • Matchday coordinator — event support, volunteer direction, and fan experience.
  • Club media assistant — social updates, photo/video support, and storytelling.

To apply, send a short message to jollyminoufc@gmail.com with your experience, role interests, and the position you are pursuing.
