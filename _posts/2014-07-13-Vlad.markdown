## About Vlad
Vlad is a technical specialist who thrives in the high-pressure environment of the midfield. Standing at 5'8", he possesses the balance and close control to navigate through crowded defenses with ease. He believes that football is a game of space and timing, and he prides himself on always being one step ahead of his marker.

His 86 Dribbling makes him an expert at carrying the ball forward from deep positions, effectively linking the defense to the front line.
