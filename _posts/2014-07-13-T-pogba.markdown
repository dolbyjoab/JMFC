## About Benviola
Wearing the **#06**, T-Pogba brings a commanding presence to the pitch. Standing at 5'11", he is a modern winger who values strength and defensive responsibility as much as attacking flair. His name is a nod to a style of play defined by technical elegance backed by raw physical power.

### Club role
T-Pogba acts as the "Enforcer on the Wing" for Jolly Minou FC. With a **79 Defense** rating, he is often tasked with neutralizing the opponent's most dangerous wide players. Once he regains possession, his **85 Pace** allows him to drive the team forward, turning a defensive stand into a lethal counter-attack in seconds.

- Playstyle: Power Winger / Defensive Specialist
- Key Strength: Physical Dominance and Ball Recovery
- Specialty: High-Press Disruptor

### Career highlights
- **Versatility King:** One of the few wingers in the league capable of elite defensive work while maintaining an **85 Pace** offensive threat.
- **Physical Landmark:** His **76 Physicality** makes him a nightmare for defenders to dispossess once he has established his stride.
